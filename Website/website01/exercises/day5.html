Exercise 1.2

Make a smrcl-script (documentation in smrcl.pdf) that starts the zoneobst cmd (as above) and logs the return variables ($l1 to $l8) Look at at logfile 'log' The smrcl commands 'log' and 'wait' may be useful.

laser "scanpush cmd='zoneobst'"
wait 0.1
log "$l0" "$l1" "$l2" "$l3" "$l4" "$l5" "$l6" "$l7" "$l8"
wait 5
stop
